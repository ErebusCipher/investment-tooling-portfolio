# Published Writing Samples

PDF writing samples selected from a broader publishing folder. The files are included as PDFs because they were already formatted for external reading and contain no obvious personal identity metadata.

## Most Relevant to the Analyst Application

- `The Agent Economy Has a Token Problem.pdf`: short thesis on why many AI-agent tokens fail to capture value from the agent economies they govern. Directly relevant to AI x crypto, autonomous agents, token value accrual, and protocol economics.
- `The Yield Trade Wearing a DeFi Costume.pdf`: RWA sector note arguing that most tokenized asset growth is currently a yield-wrapper trade rather than a composability breakthrough. Strong example of separating narrative from architecture.
- `RWA Market Monitor.pdf`: sample weekly market-monitor template for tokenized real-world assets, showing how data scaffolding and editorial judgment can be combined into a repeatable analyst workflow.

## Longer Synthesis Pieces

- `01 - The Architecture of Crypto Markets.pdf`: long-form framework treating crypto as a complex adaptive system shaped by surplus expenditure, information asymmetry, lock-in, scaling, emergence, governance constraints, and reflexivity.
- `02 - The Practice of Participating.pdf`: practitioner framework for active investing, position architecture, post-mortems, and extracting information from losses.
- `03 - Mind and Form.pdf`: broader essay on cross-disciplinary synthesis, mathematical Platonism, aesthetics, and the kind of mind required to identify structure across domains.
- `00 - Preface.pdf`: short framing note for the three-part essay sequence.
- `On the Vices of Modernity.pdf`: non-market essay included as an example of prose quality, argument structure, and willingness to state falsifiable predictions.
